Our story

Where tradition meets imagination

Melkam began as a small kitchen with a big idea: honour classic techniques while leaving room for curiosity. Today, our team sources from trusted growers and fishers, then shapes each plate with precision and heart.

The dining room is designed for unhurried evenings—soft light, honest flavours, and service that anticipates without intruding.
